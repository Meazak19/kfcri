    Internship Opportunities at KFCRI: Shaping the Future of ADR

    Kovise Foundation Conflict Resolution International (KFCRI) is a premier institution dedicated to promoting and advancing the practice of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR). Through its commitment to education, training, and professional development, KFCRI plays a vital role in shaping the future of dispute resolution in India and beyond.

    We invite students with a keen interest in law, conflict resolution, and public policy to apply for our internship program. Interns will gain exposure to the practical aspects of ADR, including arbitration, mediation, and conciliation, while working alongside experienced professionals and contributing to ongoing research and training initiatives.
