    About

    Mitesh Ravishankar is an advocate based in Chennai with experience in intellectual property, litigation, and alternative dispute resolution (ADR).

    He is a Senior Partner at MHV Partners and has been actively involved with KFCRI, serving on its Core Committee. Mitesh is also the Co-Founder and Vice Chairman of Vox Iura, an initiative dedicated to advancing legal awareness and dispute resolution. His professional journey includes roles as Legal Associate with firms such as SV&R Partners, Raj and Raj Associates, and Nathan and Associates, where he gained exposure to diverse areas of practice.

    A graduate of Dr. Ambedkar Law University’s School of Excellence in Law (B.Com LL.B.), Mitesh combines legal expertise with strong organizational and operational skills, contributing to both litigation and ADR practice.
