    About

    Hon’ble Justice Dr. P. Jyothimani served as a Judge of the Madras High Court after nearly three decades of distinguished practice in civil and appellate matters.

    A Ph.D. in Criminology from the University of Madras, he advised leading institutions including the University of Madras and Food Corporation of India, and represented prominent trusts and associations in significant cases, particularly in the field of education.

    Elevated to the High Court bench in 2005, he later served as Judicial Member of the National Green Tribunal from 2012, contributing to landmark decisions in environmental jurisprudence. His career reflects a blend of academic depth, practical expertise, and judicial leadership.
