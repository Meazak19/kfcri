    About

    Sujeet Kumar is a lawyer, social entrepreneur, and Founder of LexMantra, a full-service legal consulting firm advising corporations, multilateral agencies, investors, and individuals on legal, regulatory, and governance matters.

    He also co-founded Kalinga Kusum, a social enterprise focused on education and legal empowerment in rural communities. With over two decades of experience across corporate, government, legal, and non-profit sectors, he has held senior policy positions including Advisor to the Special Development Council of Odisha and Special Secretary of the Odisha State Planning Board, and has served as a Global Leadership Fellow at the World Economic Forum. He is currently a Member of Parliament in the Rajya Sabha representing Odisha, where he continues to advocate for bridging law, development, and social entrepreneurship.

    An alumnus of Harvard Kennedy School (MPA), Oxford University’s Said Business School (MBA), and Sambalpur University (LL.B.), Sujeet is also a trained arbitrator handling matters related to start-ups, technology, and IPR disputes, while mentoring entrepreneurs, social enterprises, and impact-driven organizations in India and abroad.
