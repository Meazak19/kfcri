    About

    Prof. Arran Dowling-Hussey is a Barrister, Arbitrator, Adjudicator, and Accredited Mediator practicing at 4–5 Gray’s Inn Square, London and in Dublin. He has acted as counsel and neutral in over 100 ADR hearings across commercial, construction, consumer, landlord–tenant, and sports law disputes. A Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, he has served as CIArb Trustee (2017–2024) and previously on its Board of Management.

    He is Co-editor of the Construction, Engineering & Energy Law Journal of Ireland and co-author of three editions of the leading Irish textbook Arbitration Law, widely cited in Irish superior court judgments. Arran has lectured extensively in arbitration and dispute resolution in Ireland, the UK, Pakistan, the UAE, and Armenia, with over 1,000 hours of teaching at third level since 2004. He is a member of the Council of the Society of Construction Law UK and the European Society of Construction Law, and has contributed to numerous professional committees including the Bar Council of Ireland ADR Committee and the Adjudication Society in Ireland.

    He is currently pursuing a part-time PhD in international arbitration at the University of Groningen and continues to combine practice, scholarship, and teaching in advancing ADR globally.
