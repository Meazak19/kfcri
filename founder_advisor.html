    Advancing Modern Dispute Resolution Through Innovation, Research, and Excellence

    Our Mission

    • Promote ADR Awareness: Promote widespread awareness and practical use of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) methods across sectors.
    • Research & Development: Undertake dedicated research and development to enhance ADR frameworks and adapt them to the evolving needs of modern economies worldwide.
    • Dynamic Rules: Design, implement, and continuously refine dynamic rules tailored to evolving ADR needs.
    • Professional Excellence: Recognize and accredit qualified professionals committed to excellence in ADR practice.

    Our Vision

    To emerge as a premier ADR institution that not only facilitates seamless and amicable dispute resolution but also sets benchmarks in accrediting competent ADR practitioners, while fostering research and development as a cornerstone of institutional excellence in ADR.
