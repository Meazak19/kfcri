About KFCRI

We're Advocates for Peace & Conflict Resolution

Arbitration

A procedure in which any dispute arising out of an agreement between parties is submitted before one or more arbitrators for a binding award..

Conciliation

A procedure in which parties of a dispute, interested in resolving the same, approach one or more conciliators to propose a settlement..

Mediation

A procedure in which parties of a dispute approach one or more mediators to bring about an amicable settlement between the parties..

Negotiation

Being an integral part of any dispute resolution, negotiation is an informal fortitude to any mechanism with its own intricate models..

Infrastructure

Highly operational facilities to be used for dispute resolution — state-of-the-art infrastructure tailored for ADR proceedings..

Rules & Regulations

Utilizable usage of progressive rules for Arbitration, Mediation and other ADR mechanisms administered by KFCRI..
