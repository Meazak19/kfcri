    Fellow at Mediation Practice

    Designatory Letters: FMP-KFCRI

    The FMP-KFCRI course offers advanced, in-depth training in mediation, combining both theoretical and practical components. It is designed to shape individuals into seasoned professionals—whether as mediators, counsel, or clients—by equipping them with the skills to navigate complex mediation processes. The training includes understanding mediation stages, applying ground rules, and creating win-win outcomes.

    Globally, the title of Fellow is recognized by KFCRI as a mark of high competence. Achieving Fellowship in Mediation signals to clients, peers, and institutions that the individual has undergone extensive training and has significant experience in the field.

    Fellows are entitled to use the designatory letters: FMP-KFCRI
