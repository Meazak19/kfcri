    About

    Adv. Devadathan V is an Advocate and ADR Practitioner, currently serving as Associate Researcher at Kovise Foundation Conflict Resolution International (KFCRI).

    A graduate of VIT University’s School of Law with a B.A., LL.B. (Hons.), he has gained professional exposure through internships with KFCRI, the Office of the Advocate General of Kerala, and the High Court of Kerala, where he developed valuable insights into arbitration, litigation, and judicial processes. At KFCRI, he contributes to legal research, drafting, and the administration of arbitral tribunals, supporting efficient dispute resolution. Beyond academics and practice, Devadathan has presented research papers at international conferences and organized events such as the International Conference on Environmental Protection and Conservation. His leadership role as Convenor of the VITSOL Moot Court Society reflects his strong organizational and communication skills.

    With a solid foundation in law, practical exposure, and a commitment to continuous learning, he is poised to make meaningful contributions to the field of arbitration and dispute resolution.
