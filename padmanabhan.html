    About

    Hon’ble Justice E. Padmanabhan retired as Judge of the Madras High Court in 2003 after a distinguished career marked by landmark judgments in constitutional, administrative, company, and environmental law. Designated Senior Advocate in 1994, he specialized in writs, service matters, and administrative law before his elevation to the bench in 1996.

    As Judge, he presided over significant cases including corruption trials, women’s workplace rights, transport regulation, and legislative powers, and contributed extensively to company and electricity law jurisprudence. He also served on the Green Bench, advancing environmental protection, and held roles such as President of the State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission and Executive Chairman of the Legal Services Authority in Pondicherry.

    Post-retirement, he has been active as an arbitrator and presiding arbitrator in domestic and international disputes, empanelled with institutions including the Indian Council of Arbitration and MCX, and appointed in high-profile matters by the Supreme Court and major corporations. His contributions extend to legal education, consumer protection, and energy law, reflecting a career dedicated to justice, reform, and dispute resolution.
