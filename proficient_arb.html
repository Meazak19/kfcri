    Proficient at Arbitration Practice

    Designatory Letters: PAP-KFCRI

    The Proficient at Arbitration Practice of KFCRI (PAP-KFCRI) is the highest level of recognition awarded by KFCRI. It is reserved for individuals who have demonstrated exceptional expertise and long-standing experience in the field of arbitration. Proficients are recognized as pioneers and thought leaders in ADR, with advanced knowledge and practical application of arbitration law.

    Achieving this status signifies mastery in arbitration and a commitment to excellence. It is a mark of distinction that sets you apart in the global ADR community.

    Proficients are entitled to use the designatory letters: PAP-KFCRI

    Subscription Details

    Eligibility Criteria

    Category 1: Holders of the Fellow Certificate from KFCRI who have successfully cleared an interview conducted by the KFCRI Interview Board.
    Category 2: Professionals with 15+ years of experience in arbitration (with references).
