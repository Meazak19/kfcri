About KFCRI

Kovise Foundation Conflict Resolution International

KFCRI — Kovise Foundation Conflict Resolution International — is a first-of-its-kind institution in India established in 2016, dedicated to promoting Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) across all sectors of the economy. KFCRI serves as a comprehensive platform providing ADR Practice Accreditation, a fully functional Dispute Resolution Centre, capacity-building programmes, international seminars, webinars, and awareness initiatives to empower practitioners and clients alike.

Arbitration

A formal procedure in which a dispute arising out of an agreement between parties is submitted before one or more arbitrators, who render a binding and enforceable award.

Conciliation

A procedure in which parties to a dispute approach one or more conciliators who actively assist in proposing and facilitating an agreed settlement between them.

Mediation

A voluntary procedure in which parties to a dispute approach one or more mediators who guide the parties toward an amicable, mutually agreeable settlement.

Negotiation

An integral and foundational part of every dispute resolution process — negotiation is an informal yet structured discipline with its own intricate models and strategies.

Dispute Resolution Centre

KFCRI operates a fully functional Dispute Resolution Centre with state-of-the-art hearing rooms, administrative support, and online facilities for ADR proceedings.

Rules & Regulations

KFCRI administers its own progressive and internationally aligned rules for Arbitration, Mediation, Conciliation, and Negotiation proceedings.
