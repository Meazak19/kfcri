    About

    Surya Narmada Inbavijayan is the Founder of Kovise Foundation Conflict Resolution International (KFCRI), a leading institution dedicated to promoting alternative dispute resolution (ADR) worldwide. She is also a horticulturist, agriculture arbitration consultant, and government-registered valuer for agricultural lands.

    Holding a postgraduate degree in horticulture from Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, she brings over two decades of expertise as a consultant, trainer, and advisor in agriculture and horticulture. Surya is the Proprietor of Kovise Agro, an agribusiness venture connecting farmers and producer companies directly with consumers, and the Founder and Managing Trustee of Kovise Foundation, which works to empower rural communities through knowledge and social development. In addition, she is a Partner at Kove Global LLP, an arbitration and ADR boutique specializing in agriculture and commodity disputes.

    Her professional strengths include farm planning, orchard management, landscape design, and therapeutic horticulture, complemented by extensive training programs delivered to NGOs, universities, and rural communities. A Fellow of the Institution of Valuers, she continues to drive innovation in agriculture, dispute resolution, and sustainable development.
