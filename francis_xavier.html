    About

    Francis Xavier is a Senior Counsel (appointed in 2009) and Regional Head of Disputes Practice, specializing in international arbitration, treaty arbitration, and complex commercial litigation.

    He has acted in high-profile cross-border disputes in banking, finance, property, and energy, including the first case heard before the Singapore International Commercial Court. Recognized as a leading lawyer by AsiaLaw Profiles, Legal 500, Chambers Global, Who’s Who Legal, and Benchmark Litigation Asia-Pacific, he has also advised on landmark aviation cases such as the SilkAir and SIA crash litigations.

    Francis sits as a party-nominated arbitrator in ICSID treaty disputes, assists Asian governments in developing arbitration frameworks, and serves in leadership roles as Chartered Arbitrator, immediate Past Chairman of CIArb Singapore, President-Elect of the Inter-Pacific Bar Association, and Vice President of CIArb (UK). His career reflects deep expertise in dispute resolution and international arbitration leadership.
