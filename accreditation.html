    “Usus Magister Est Optimus” – Practice is the Best Teacher

    Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) has existed long before formal court systems, with communities using various methods to resolve conflicts peacefully. Today, ADR continues to evolve—and KFCRI is proud to be at the forefront of this movement.

    At Kovise Foundation Conflict Resolution International (KFCRI) , we are committed to recognizing and empowering professionals in the ADR space. Our accreditation process is designed to equip individuals with the skills, recognition, and opportunities they need to thrive in the field of dispute resolution.
